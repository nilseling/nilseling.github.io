About me

I'm a postdoctoral reseracher at the Bodenmiller laboratory at the University of Zurich.

My research focuses on understanding the molecular changes over breast cancer organoid growth. I'm integrating imaging mass cytometry with single-cell based statistical approaches to model spatial heterogeneity in organoids.
